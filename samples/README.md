# Sample Clips

## Recommended Sample Clips

For the first sample, prepare two short clips.

### Master Clip

10-20 seconds.

Shows:

- stable hand pressure
- centered object or material
- light touch
- calm timing
- no face visible

### Apprentice Clip

10-20 seconds.

Shows:

- too much pressure
- visible wobble or mistake
- unstable movement
- rushed timing
- no face visible

### Master Hint

Do not force the clay. Keep your hands steady, use light pressure, and wait until the center becomes stable.

## If pottery clips are not available

Use a small practice setup:

- paper clay or oil clay
- rotating plate or cake turntable
- smartphone camera
- 45-degree overhead angle
- hands only
- no face

The goal is not perfect pottery.
The goal is to demonstrate expert-beginner difference capture.

## Other sample ideas

- knife cutting technique
- cleaning motion
- tool handling
- brush stroke
- packaging work
- coffee pour
- repair task
- machine operation gesture
- sports hand movement

The product should support many skill domains, not only pottery.
